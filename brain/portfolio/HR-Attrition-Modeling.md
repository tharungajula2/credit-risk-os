# HR Attrition & Performance Analytics

Applying quantitative risk techniques to human resources. This project treats employee attrition as a predictable risk event, allowing HR departments to deploy targeted retention strategies.

## Project Architecture
- **Objective:** Identify the core drivers of employee turnover and predict individual attrition risks.
- **Core Methodology:** Built and evaluated an ensemble of predictive models, including Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost.
- **Outcomes:** The optimized XGBoost model achieved a remarkable **96% accuracy**. More importantly, SHAP values and feature importance extraction provided actionable, data-driven HR insights (e.g., identifying salary, commute distance, or management changes as primary flight risks).
